I am making a character called Vento Solevino for a video game that I will help design later in the year. The way that I am making
this character is by 3d modelling it in blender as well as rigging and animating it there as wellVento is an Artificer (Magical Engineer)
who specialises on making
magical firearms and cannons. He is from the fictional country of the menagirie coast and is from the
tinkerer town of Port Zoon. Vento is an Air Genasi which means he has some essence of the elemental plane of Air in him. This gives him
certain abilites such as producing air inside of his lungs and having minor control over air and lightning.
